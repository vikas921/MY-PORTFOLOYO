Pollution Causes

Land Pollution Water Pollution Air Pollution
Land polution is the deterioration (destruction) of the earth's
land surfaces,often directly or indirectly as result of man's
activaties and their misuse of land resources.
Water Pollution is the contamination of water bodies
(e.g. lakes,rivers,oceans,aquifers and grounwater).
very often by human activities.
Air Pollution (say:po-loo-shun)occur when gases, dust particles,
fumes (or smoke)or odour are introduced into the atmosphere in a way
that makes it harmful to humans, animals and plant.
